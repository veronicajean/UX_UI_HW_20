Wedo

Wedo is your friendly helper to a cleaner home. We assist you in organizing chores and allocating tasks with others in your household.

About the Project



THE PROBLEM:

People struggle with allocating household tasks, making tasks fair for everyone in the household, all while minimizing friction.


THE SOLUTION:

Wedo a task management app that allows housemates to delegate tasks fairly, complete tasks efficiently and reduce friction in the household.

Takeaways & Next Steps


My team and I were able to create an application prototype to help people allocate tasks with housemates. The interface is simple with fun bright colors while at the same time being intuitive and easy to use with the navigation. This idea can help resolve difficult decisions with roommates as well as makes a common issue less stressful & more fair

We know this app is not perfect, we will continue to iterate and improve it’s features. Future ideas we have to add onto this app are in-app messaging systems, more content on the homepage, and expanded options on chores.
